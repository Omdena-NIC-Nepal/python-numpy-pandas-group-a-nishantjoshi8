Children and Youth at the Center of Nepal’s Climate Change Agenda
“We demand an environment conducive to ensuring climate justice and building a climate-resilient society.”
In the dialogue, children, adolescents and youth shared their first-hand experiences on climate change and the efforts they are making at their level.
The declaration includes their demands, commitments and proposed actions for building a climate-resilient Nepal.
